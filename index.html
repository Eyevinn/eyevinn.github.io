Eyevinn Technology Open Source Software Center

We are the leading independent consultant firm specializing in video technology and media distribution. Based in Stockholm and as a team we have the combined knowledge on how to build a large scale video streaming service and our consultants are working with large broadcasters, TV operators, newspapers and other media companies in Sweden and the Nordics.

We want to provide the viewer with the best possible experience when watching video and TV on the Internet and believe that contributing to the open source community and share our knowledge in this technology will contribute to building even better viewer experiences.

This is our contribution back to the open source community.

Our Manifesto

Our manifesto is that whenever we have the possibility to release our code as open source we should. Source code under non-disclosure agreement and where the intellectual property belongs to our customers are of course not released here. We have recurring Hackdays where the team develop proof-of-concepts or tools that goes directly in here.

Featured Content

  • channel-engine The Eyevinn Channel Engine is a microservice that offers the functionality to generate personalized live streams from available VOD content.
  • vod-to-live.js A NodeJS library to generate live HLS from VOD
  • ott-multiview A web based multiview screen for HLS and MPEG-DASH streams
  • docker-toolbelt A Docker container filled with pre-built versions of video streaming related tools
