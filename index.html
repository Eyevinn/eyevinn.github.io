Eyevinn Technology

We are a Swedish based consultancy company with consultants sharing a passion for the technology that makes it possible to watch TV on Internet.

Follow us on GitHub

We are a consultancy company based in Stockholm, Sweden, specialized in video streaming technology. As a team we have the combined knowledge on how to build a large scale video streaming service and our consultants are working with large broadcasters, TV operators, newspapers and other media companies in Sweden and the Nordics.

This is our contribution back to the open source community.

Our Manifesto

Our manifesto is that whenever we have the possibility to release our code as open source we should. Source code under non-disclosure agreement and where the intellectual property belongs to our customers are of course not released here. We have recurring Hackdays where the team develop proof-of-concepts or tools that goes directly in here.

Featured Content

  • docker-toolbelt A Docker container filled with pre-built versions of video streaming related tools
  • html5-dash-player HTML5 player and analyzer for MPEG-DASH streams
  • html5-hls-player HTML5 player and analyzer for HLS streams
  • codem-client Watch-folder based ABR-transcoding based on FFMpeg
